# Contributing to Príncipe

Thanks for stopping by. The project is in active early-stage development — here's what's open and what isn't right now.

## What's open

- **Issues.** Bug reports against the design or docs, feature ideas, install-story pain points, persona/region coverage suggestions — file freely.
- **Discussions.** Strategy questions, panel-composition ideas, sources for the knowledge base, what-if scenarios.
- **Feedback on the public roadmap** (see [README.md](README.md)).

## What's paused

- **Pull requests.** The codebase is mid-migration from its SaaS-era monorepo. Outside PRs can't land cleanly until Sprint 8 stabilizes the structure (~July 2026). We'll flip this section the moment that's done.

## When PRs reopen

- One topic per PR. Big refactors split into reviewable chunks.
- Tests pass locally: `pnpm test` (or whatever Sprint 8 settles on).
- Conventional commit style: `feat(panel): ...`, `fix(statistician): ...`, `docs: ...`.
- AGPL-3.0 sign-off in the commit body: `Signed-off-by: Your Name <you@example.com>`. By signing off you confirm you have the right to contribute the change under AGPL-3.0.

## Code of conduct

Be kind, be specific, assume good faith. We'll formalize a CoC when PRs reopen.

— Omer
